About Me

Who am I?

Hello there! I'm an 18-year-old trainspotter with a deep interest in the world of trains. My fascination with trains began at a young age but faded for a while. When I started traveling by train more frequently, my interest returned and has stayed ever since.

My favorite locomotives from NMBS are the HLE 18/19 and the HLE 27. Outside of NMBS, I have a preference for the Siemens Vectron.

In addition to trains, I enjoy photographing animals, buildings, sunsets and nature.

I am currently a trainee train driver at NMBS, following the official training program to become a certified train driver.

Follow my journey as I continue to explore and document the world of trains.
